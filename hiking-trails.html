Hiking Trails

Explore some of the best hiking trails near Sacramento, each offering unique scenery and varying difficulty levels.

Hikes Near Sacramento

  • American River Parkway: A multi-use trail running alongside the American River, perfect for hiking, biking, and running.
  • Effie Yeaw Nature Center: An easy trail that's great for families and offers plenty of wildlife viewing opportunities.
  • Darrington Trail: A scenic trail that offers views of Folsom Lake, particularly beautiful in the spring.
  • Hidden Falls Regional Park: Features over 30 miles of multi-use trails with waterfalls and beautiful vistas.
  • Stevens Trail: Known for its breathtaking views of the North Fork American River and wildflowers in the spring.
