Basic Sourdough Bread

Ingredients:

  • 100 grams sourdough starter
  • 350 grams of warm water
  • 500 grams bread flour
  • 7-9 grams of teaspoons salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sourdough starter, warm water, and flour
  2. Then add the salt. Mix until the dough begins to come together.
  3. Once you see that there is no left over flour and all incoropated let sit for 30 minutes to an hour.
  4. After you let it rest you will now start your stretch and folds (you'll do this 3-4 times).
  5. I like to place a towel over the bowl or you can use a towel (your personal preference).
  6. With your stretch and fold youll pull one side of the dough up and flip ir over the rest of the dough do this 4 times rotating the bowl.
  7. Make sure to do this 30 minutes to 1 hour apart.
  8. After the 4 stretch and folds then youll let it rest for about 6 hours but it depends on how warm it is in your house.
  9. You'll then do your do your final proof in the fridge for no less than 12 hours in a well floured proofing bowl with a shower cap.
  10. After the 12 hours youre ready to bake!
  11. Preheat oven to 450 degrees farenheit and place the dutch oven inside to preheat as well
  12. Then once preheated for about 30 minutes take dutch oven out and place sourdough on parchment paper and score
  13. Bake with lid on for 30 minutes
  14. Then take lid off and bake for about 40 minutes
  15. Once done, take out of oven and let cool for about an hour or 2 before cutting into
  16. Last step is to enjoy!!
